ABOUT THE PROGRAM

Preparing Future Healthcare & Business Leaders Through Strategic Management & Innovation

The Management & Entrepreneurship programs at NIPAS (BS Public Health and BS Healthcare Management) are designed to prepare visionary, strategic, and ethical leaders capable of managing healthcare organizations, driving public health initiatives, and creating sustainable solutions in the healthcare sector.

Students gain a strong foundation in management principles, healthcare policy, entrepreneurship, leadership, and business strategies while developing the skills needed to address complex challenges in modern healthcare systems.

Leadership Development

Build essential leadership, communication and decision-making skills.

Strategic Management

Learn healthcare administration, policy, and business strategies.

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Develop skills to launch and manage healthcare ventures.

Global Opportunities

Prepare for national and international leadership roles.
