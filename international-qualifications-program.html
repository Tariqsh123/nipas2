ABOUT THE PROGRAM

Preparing Globally Competitive Professionals Through International Standards & Pathways

The International Qualifications programs at NIPAS offer Pearson BTEC, Higher National Certificates (HNC), Higher National Diplomas (HND), and structured progression pathways. These qualifications are designed to meet global academic and industry standards.

Students benefit from a practical, career-oriented curriculum that facilitates seamless transfer to top universities worldwide and prepares them for successful international careers.
