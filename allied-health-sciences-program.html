ABOUT THE PROGRAM

Preparing Future Healthcare Professionals Through Practical Training & Innovation

The Allied Health Sciences programs at NIPAS are designed to prepare competent, skilled, and compassionate healthcare professionals in specialized fields including Medical Laboratory Technology (MLT), Anaesthesia, Radiography, Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT), Nutrition, Dental Technology, and Cosmetology.

Students receive extensive hands-on laboratory training, clinical exposure, and modern technical education to meet the growing demand for allied health professionals in hospitals, diagnostic centers, rehabilitation facilities, and wellness industries.

Hands-on Lab Training

State-of-the-art laboratories and practical skill development.

Clinical & Technical Skills

Build expertise in specialized healthcare procedures and technologies.

Modern Equipment & Technology

Global Career Opportunities

Prepare for national and international roles in healthcare.
